Putting God First — "Una ang Ginoo"

Personal reflections about faith, service, and how we use technology with integrity.

Faith encourages us to use our gifts responsibly. In the digital world, we show integrity by sharing truthful messages, respecting others’ dignity, and using technology to uplift rather than harm.

When we say Una ang Ginoo, we remind ourselves to put the values of compassion, honesty, and service before personal gain — even online. Consider small acts: a kind comment, properly crediting sources, or volunteering to help a community project.

Reflection prompts

  • How can I use my online presence to spread encouragement?
  • What responsibilities do I have when sharing information?
  • How can technology help my community in practical ways?
